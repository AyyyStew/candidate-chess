# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: March 2026_

Candidate Chess ("the app", "we", "us") is a hobby project. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. What We Collect

Account data: When you sign in via Google, we receive your email address and display name. When you sign in via Lichess, we receive your Lichess username and a system-generated Lichess email address. In both cases, this data is used solely to create your account and preserve your streak across sessions.

Gameplay data: We collect your guesses, puzzle results, solve history, and related game telemetry.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your account identifiers are used only for authentication, to associate your game history with your account, and to power your personal stats dashboard. We do not use them for advertising, marketing, or third-party analytics tools.
Gameplay data (guesses, solves, streaks, puzzle history, etc.) may be aggregated, analyzed, and published publicly. Any data shared or published will not include your email address or account name.

3. Third-Party Sign-In

We use Google and Lichess for authentication. By signing in through either provider, you are also subject to their respective privacy policies. We do not control the data they collect during the sign-in process.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account and gameplay data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from your account page. We will remove your data when reasonably possible, but as a hobby project running on a best-effort basis, we cannot guarantee a specific timeframe.

5. Children

This app is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

6. Contact

This is an open-source project. For questions or concerns, you can open an issue on our public GitHub repository.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we materially change this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
